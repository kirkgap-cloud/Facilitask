System Policy

Goal of the System: This system is designed to help students manage their tasks efficiently, track their progress, and store completed work for future reference.

What to Do:

  • Use the Work List to add, update, and complete tasks.
  • Upload proof of task completion before marking it as done.
  • View past completed tasks in the History section.

Acceptance: By clicking "Accept Policy," you agree to follow the guidelines outlined above and use the system responsibly.
